Mango Daiquiri

The hell is a Mango Daiquiri

Stolen From WikipediaThe daiquiri is a cocktail whose main ingredients are rum, citrus juice, and sugar or other sweetener. The daiquiri is one of the six basic drinks listed in David A. Embury's classic The Fine Art of Mixing Drinks, which also lists some variations. This time, it's mango time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 2 fluid ounces rum
  • 2 fluid ounces freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 fluid ounces agave syrup

HOW DO YA MAKE IT?!

  1. Place mangoes, rum, lime juice, and agave syrup in your blender cup.
  2. Blend that shit until it is smooth.
  3. Don't chug cause that would cause brain freeze. You don't want that or do you?
